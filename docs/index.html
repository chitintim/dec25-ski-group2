Why La Tania?

Perfect Base

Quiet village between Courchevel & Méribel. Quick gondola to Courchevel 1850, ski down the blue run home.

3 Valleys Access

600km of pistes, 328 runs, altitude up to 3,230m. The world's largest ski area at your doorstep.

Village Charm

Car-free centre, authentic atmosphere, good restaurants, 25 mins from Courchevel's après-ski.
