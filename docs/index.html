Why La Tania?

Having spent countless weeks exploring the 3 Valleys, I can confidently say La Tania is the perfect base for our trip. It's a charming, car-free village that offers the best of both worlds: quick access to the entire 3 Valleys ski area whilst maintaining a peaceful, authentic Alpine atmosphere.

Perfect Location

  • Strategic Position: Nestled between Courchevel and Méribel, giving easy access to all 600km of the 3 Valleys
  • Quick Links: The gondola takes you straight up to Courchevel 1850 in minutes
  • Ski-in Access: The Morettaz Blanche blue run leads right into the village
  • Peaceful Setting: Away from the crowds but just 25 minutes from Courchevel's famous après-ski

The 3 Valleys Advantage

With your lift pass, you'll have access to:

  • 600km of interconnected pistes
  • 328 ski runs across all levels
  • Altitudes up to 3,230m (guaranteed snow)
  • Three distinct valleys to explore
  • World-class ski schools and facilities
