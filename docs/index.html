Why La Tania?

Well guys, for those who know me, you know I'm obsessed with this place...

After 20+ weeks here, I can tell you La Tania is the sweet spot of the 3 Valleys. It's budget-friendly access to Courchevel (that gondola gets you to 1850 in minutes!), but without the crazy prices and crowds. The skiing here is so much quieter than the other resorts - you'll actually have space to carve!
